arsenal_gear.stellar_evolution.utils.make_monotonic_decreasing¶

arsenal_gear.stellar_evolution.utils.make_monotonic_decreasing(x: float64, y: float64) → float64[source]¶

Make an array montonically decreasing by replacing increasing values with interpolated values based on the nearest by points j and k such that x[j] < x[k] and y[j] > y[k]. Args:

x (np.float64): indepednent variable, used for interpolation y (np.float64): dependent variable to be made monotonic
